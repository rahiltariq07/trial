Secure
Yoroi Wallet is a self custodial open-source light wallet built in the open for the Cardano ecosystem. The level of transparency enhances the security. Anyone may explore or inspect the Yoroi source code on Github. Private keys are encrypted, and never shared with our servers or third party providers.
Fast
Quickly access your ADA, NFTs, and Cardano Native Assets through your mobile device and/or browser extension. Yoroi’s lightweight design empowers you to seamlessly engage with the Cardano Web3 ecosystem—including DEXs, DeFi platforms, and NFTs—without the burden of downloading the full blockchain.
Simple
We believe that software should have a simple structure and a beautiful user interface. Developed by EMURGO Fintech a division within EMURGO – a founding entity of Cardano blockchain – Yoroi was the first light wallet supporting Cardano ADA. We’ve been providing users with intuitive, scalable and robust innovation since 2018.
